Microsoft Copilot Chat

AI-powered chat assistant available to all Defra staff for general productivity and assistance.

Good news: Microsoft Copilot Chat is already available to you through your Microsoft 365 account. You can start using it today at copilot.microsoft.com or in Microsoft Teams.

What is Copilot Chat?

Microsoft Copilot Chat is an AI assistant that can help with a wide range of tasks using natural language conversation. It's integrated with your Microsoft 365 environment and designed to enhance your productivity.

What you can use it for

Writing and communication

  • Draft emails and documents
  • Improve writing style and tone
  • Summarize long texts
  • Create meeting agendas and notes

Research and analysis

  • Research topics and gather information
  • Analyze data and trends
  • Generate ideas and solutions
  • Answer questions and explain concepts

How to access Copilot Chat

  1. Web browser: Go to copilot.microsoft.com and sign in with your Defra account
  2. Microsoft Teams: Look for the Copilot app in your Teams sidebar
  3. Office apps: Find Copilot features integrated into Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Outlook

Using Copilot Chat responsibly

! Warning Do not share Official Sensitive information, personal data, or confidential details in your conversations with Copilot Chat.

Best practices

  • Check data classification - Only use Public or Internal information
  • Verify outputs - Always review and fact-check AI-generated content
  • Protect privacy - Don't include names, addresses, or personal details
  • Stay professional - Use Copilot for work-related tasks only
  • Be critical - AI can make mistakes or provide outdated information

What not to use it for

  • Processing Official Sensitive or personal data
  • Making final decisions without human review
  • Accessing or sharing confidential information
  • Creating content that could be misleading or harmful

Privacy and data protection

Your conversations with Copilot Chat are subject to Microsoft's enterprise privacy controls:

  • Conversations are not used to train AI models
  • Data is processed within your organization's Microsoft 365 tenant
  • Standard Microsoft 365 data governance applies
  • Audit logs are maintained for compliance
